App Summary Prompt — HVAC On-Demand Service Platform
Platform Name: (TBD — placeholder: "ServiceConnect") Type: Web + Mobile App (iOS/Android) Model: Uber Eats-style on-demand HVAC dispatch marketplace

Overview
A two-sided marketplace connecting homeowners and property managers with verified, certified HVAC technicians. Customers post service requests at preset prices; registered techs claim jobs first come first serve. The platform handles dispatch, real-time tracking, escrow payments, tax automation for techs, advertising slots, and a product marketplace — all under one roof.

HVAC Tech Path
Registration & Onboarding (similar to UberEats driver signup):

Techs must submit the following before activation:

•
Government-issued Driver's License (front & back photo upload)
•
IRS Form W-9 (upload or fill out in-app)
•
1099-NEC Acknowledgment — tech agrees they are an independent contractor and will receive a 1099-NEC at year end
•
HVAC Certification(s) — upload copies of relevant certifications (EPA 608, state licenses, NATE, etc.)
•
Service Coverage Area — zip codes or radius they operate in
•
Bank account / direct deposit info for payouts
All documents reviewed by admin before account activation. Status shown as: Pending Review → Approved → Active

Platform Fee Agreement (shown during signup as a contract/terms):

Per-job platform fee: [X]% of each service call (e.g., 15%) — OR —
•

Both options clearly outlined in a scrollable contract the tech e-signs before activation
•
Fee structure shown on every job offer so techs see their net payout before accepting

Job Workflow for Tech:

•
Receive push notification for nearby job
•
View job details: service type, address (partially masked until accepted), offered payout (after platform fee)
•
Tap "Accept Job" — locked to that tech, removed from pool
•
Navigate via in-app map to customer
•
Mark "Arrived"
•
Complete job → upload required completion photos
•
Mark "Job Complete" → triggers customer confirmation → funds released to tech's account
•
Payout processed within 2–3 business days (or instant for subscription tier)
